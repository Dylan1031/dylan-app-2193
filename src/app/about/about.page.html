About Vision The Alliance of Christian Churches in Namibia is envisioned to be a group of Christian Leaders in Namibia that convenes to deliberate and address issues of National importance as the prophetic voice of God, to the Church and the Nation at large. Mission To give guidance and council to the Church and Nation at large on issues related to the spiritual wellbeing, healing, restoration, welfare and development of the Nation of Namibia. To engage relevant stakeholders such as the Government of the Republic of Namibia, Politicians, and Private and Public Institutions on issues that affect the social and moral fabric of the nation.
